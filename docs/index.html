Veil enforces ad frequency caps using differential privacy math. The same ad can't follow you everywhere — and your identity stays yours.

Works on top of your ad blocker. Catches what it misses — YouTube, social feeds, sites that fight blockers. Your real counts never leave your device.

How Veil works
Step 1
Detect
Spots ad slots that got past your blocker — YouTube, social feeds, news sites.
Step 2
Count
Tracks how many times each ad appeared. Count stays on your device only.
Step 3
Scramble
Adds mathematical noise before any data leaves your device. Server sees a blurred number.
Step 4
Block
Once an ad crosses your limit, Veil suppresses it. Works even when your blocker is off.
